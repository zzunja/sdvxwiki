## Intro

This page contains course information and unlock conditions for the 11th and ∞ Dan courses of SDVX EXCEED GEAR. Other levels are not included as their courses can be attempted without specific song unlocks.

Related information:
  - 11th Dan is unlocked after clearing one 10th dan course
  - ∞ Dan is unlocked after clearing one 11th dan course
  - For Konasute (コナステ), courses will not appear at all if you do not own the song pack that a song belongs to
  - Unlock systems referenced in this page:
    - PCB (in-game currency)
    - [BLASTER GATE](/compendium/unlocks#blaster-gate)
    - [ΩDimension](/compendium/unlocks#omega-dimension)
    - [HEXA DIVER](/compendium/unlocks#hexadiver)
  
For information about how dan courses work, see [this page](/compendium/gamemodes#skill-analyzer).

For course information for 10th Dan and below, see this [RemyWiki page](https://remywiki.com/SKILL_ANALYZER).
