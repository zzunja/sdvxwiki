## Intro

This page contains course information and unlock conditions for the 11th and ∞ Dan courses of SOUND VOLTEX ∇ (Nabla). Other levels are not included as their courses can be attempted without specific song unlocks.

Related information:
  - 11th Dan is unlocked after clearing one 10th Dan course
  - ∞ Dan is unlocked after clearing one 11th Dan course
  - For Konasute (コナステ), courses will not appear at all if you do not own the song pack that a song belongs to
  - Unlock systems referenced in this page:
    - PCB (in-game currency)
    - [BLASTER GATE](/compendium/unlock-systems#blaster-gate)
  
For information about how dan courses work, see [this page](/compendium/game-modes#skill-analyzer).

For course information for 10th Dan and below, see this [RemyWiki page](https://remywiki.com/SKILL_ANALYZER).
